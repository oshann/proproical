Notes & Assumptions

  • Calculations: Default rates are based on the 2024 Malaysian Budget and the Solicitors' Remuneration Order (SRO) 2023.
  • Entry Fees: State Levy, MOT, and Legal Fees are calculated as a percentage of the Property Price.
  • Resale vs New: Stamp Duty (MOT) for Resale properties follows a tiered scale (1% - 4%). For New developments, these are often absorbed by developers (set to 0% default).
  • Occupancy Assumption: Rental income assumes a 100% occupancy rate.
  • Investment Verdict: The Poor/Fair/Good/Great label above is a quick heuristic based on Gross Rental Yield and Monthly Cashflow only — it does not account for appreciation, location, or your personal financial situation, and is not financial advice.
  • Financial Disclaimer: This calculator is only a guide. Results vary according to different financial institutions. PropROICal doesn't guarantee its accuracy or applicability to your circumstances.

Projection Notes & Estimations

  • Property Value Estimation: Calculations use a phased appreciation multiplier based on historical trends
  • Occupancy Assumption: Rental income assumes a 100% occupancy rate.
  • Net Profit/Lost: Logic used: (Estimated Property Value + Accumulated Rental Income) - (Remaining Loan Balance + Accumulated Expenses + Initial Down Payment + Entry Fees + Setup Costs).
  • Exclusions: These figures do not include agent commissions from leasing or selling, or other potential costs/fees involved in property transactions.
