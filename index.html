Notes & Assumptions

  • Calculations: Default rates are based on the 2024 Malaysian Budget and the Solicitors' Remuneration Order (SRO) 2023.
  • Entry Fees: State Levy, MOT, and Legal Fees are calculated as a percentage of the Property Price.
  • Resale vs New: Stamp Duty (MOT) for Resale properties follows a tiered scale (1% - 4%). For New developments, these are often absorbed by developers (set to 0% default).
  • Occupancy Assumption: Rental income assumes a 100% occupancy rate.
  • Financial Disclaimer: This calculator is only a guide. Results vary according to different financial institutions. PropROICal doesn't guarantee its accuracy or applicability to your circumstances.

Projection Notes & Estimations
