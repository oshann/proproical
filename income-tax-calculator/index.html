How This Is Calculated (YA2025)

  • Chargeable Income: Annual Income minus Personal Relief (RM9,000, automatic) minus EPF Relief (up to RM4,000) minus Spouse Relief (RM4,000, if claimed) minus Child Relief (RM2,000 per child under 18) minus Other Reliefs.
  • Tax Brackets: LHDN's YA2025 progressive rates, 0% to 30%, applied to chargeable income (unchanged from YA2024).
  • RM400 Rebate: Applied automatically when chargeable income is RM35,000 or below.
  • Simplifications: Child relief above uses the basic under-18 rate only (tertiary-education and disabled-child reliefs can be higher -- add the difference under "Other Reliefs"). This tool does not enumerate all ~20 LHDN relief categories individually.
  • Disclaimer: This calculator is a guide only. For an exact figure, use LHDN's official e-Filing system or consult a licensed tax agent.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are Malaysia's income tax rates for YA2025?

Progressive rates from 0% to 30%, unchanged from YA2024. See the full bracket breakdown above once you enter your income.

What is chargeable income?

Your annual income minus allowable reliefs. Tax is calculated on chargeable income, not your gross salary.

Does this include every tax relief?

No -- it covers the most common ones and a lump-sum "other reliefs" field. For an exact figure, use LHDN's e-Filing system.
